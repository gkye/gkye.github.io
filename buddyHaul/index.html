What is BuddyHaul?

BuddyHaul is an online marketplace connecting people in need of an on-demand moving and delivery service with a Buddy that has a vehicle to get the job done. BuddyHaul provides an alternative to expensive and inconvenient truck rentals and delivery/moving companies, with a convenient, affordable, and community-oriented delivery service. We are a Canadian company currently servicing the GTA area. However, we are expanding and will be launching our services nationwide.
